The Great CUSFS Hall of Fame Vote 2003/4

Finally revived after several years of neglect, the CUSFS Hall of Fame returns. Twelve years ago, the results looked like this: results 1991-2. Of course, a lot has changed since then. Hundreds of new authors, thousands and thousands of new books. Not to mention the small matter of merging with Jómsborg the New, the fantasy society. How have the results changed? Here's how people voted...
