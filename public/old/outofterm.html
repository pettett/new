CUSFS - Out of Term

Outside Full Term, CUSFS members often meet informally on Thursday evenings. The venue is often the Carlton Arms (on Carlton Way), but please contact the CUSFS committee using soc-cusfs at lists dot cam dot ac dot uk for confirmation.

The programme for the coming term is not yet available - it is likely to be announced in e-mail missives and on this website nearer the time.

Details of past terms' programmes are available in the CUSFS archive.

Last update: 3 July 2005, by Mark Waller
