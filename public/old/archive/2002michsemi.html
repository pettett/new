CUSFS Semi-detached Events - Michaelmas Term 2002

Unofficial events to which CUSFS members are welcome.

Stargate videos

Time: 8.00pm or 9.00pm prompt (see below)
Venue: 61 Green End Road

This is not an official CUSFS event, but CUSFS people are welcome (several are already going) and we thought you might be interested.

We're going to show season 1 of Stargate at Greenend (61 Green End Road) over the next few weeks on Wednesday evenings starting at 9pm, watching four episodes per evening (i.e. finishing at midnight). If you turn up at 8pm then you can also watch the season 6 episode being shown on Sky One. If you want to catch everything then don't be late - we won't wait for anyone!

The schedule:

The Broca Divide, The First Commandment, The Enemy Within, Emancipation

20th November:
Brief Candle, Thor's Hammer, The Torment of Tantalus, Cold Lazarus

Enigma, Solitudes, Tin Man, There But For The Grace Of God, Politics, Within the Serpent's Grasp (cliffhanger)

 
Semi-detached games evenings

Time and venue: 7.30pm at the Castle Inn for food or 9.00pm at Relativity (155 Gilbert Road)

Another unofficial event: a group of CUSFS people often meet in the Castle (on Castle Hill) on a Thursday evening for food, and then retire to Relativity for coffee, games and booze.

Typically, games played can include Mao (an intriguing and mysterious card game), Albatross (a locally-developed Whist variant with bidding), Pairs Gnostica (a strategy game played with tarot cards and small pointy pyramids!) and others.

You'd be welcome to join us. Note also that the official CUSFS games evening will be taking place on Sunday 10th November, and a wider variety of games will be played there (and bringing your own is encouraged), so if you only turn up to one games evening please come to the official one!
