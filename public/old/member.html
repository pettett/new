CUSFS Membership

The benefits of CUSFS membership include:
  • weekly discussions on authors and other topics related to SF and fantasy
  • meetings with invited speakers
  • borrowing rights for the CUSFS library (over 6,000 books and magazines)
  • free supply of the CUSFS magazine, TTBA
Membership of CUSFS is available at the following rates:
  • 1 year: £4.00
  • 2 years: £7.00
  • 3 years: £10.00
  • generally, n years for £(3n + 1) at the discretion of the Membership Secretary
If you have any questions, or would like to join CUSFS, please e-mail the Committee on soc-cusfs at lists dot cam dot ac dot uk, or contact the Treasurer or the Membership Secretary at any meeting.
