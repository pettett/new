Cambridge University Science Fiction Society

and Jómsborg the New




CUSFS (and later Jómsborg) has been bringing literary discussion of Science Fiction and Fantasy to students and the general public in Cambridge since 1963. Every week during University Full Term we have a discussion on Sunday evenings, be it on an author, a theme, or the latest bizarre theory explaining why there's nothing good on TV anymore. We tend to alternate between Science Fiction discussions and Fantasy discussions (which, until 1996, were the exclusive preserve of Jómsborg).

If you would like more information on what CUSFS does, then contact the committee at soc-cusfs at lists dot cam dot ac dot uk
