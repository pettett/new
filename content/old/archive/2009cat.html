The CUSFS Library Catalogue

This is the catalogue of the library of the CU Science Fiction Society. At present we have a little over 5000 books. Borrowing is free but limited to CUSFS members. See the membership page for details of how to join.

We are currently having a library sale, now open to non-members as well as members.

If there's something here you want to borrow, simply email the Librarian at soc-cusfs-library at lists dot cam dot ac dot uk to arrange a time to collect it. Please cut-and-paste the relevant catalogue lines into your mail if you can (including all the numbers etc.) as this helps with fetching the books.

Books marked 'out' are currently on loan; books marked 'X' are being repaired. These can be borrowed eventually but may take a while to become available. Please ask if you're interested in something as this will probably accelerate the process!

The best way to borrow or return library books is at discussions/events *with advance warning*. Other things are possible but may not be very efficient.

See also What's New?

There is also a mostly-complete list of our infinite Star Trek collection by number rather than author.

Key to letters:
A = anthology - D = double - N = non-fiction
X = temporarily withdrawn for repair
